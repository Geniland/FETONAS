Parallax

Experience depth and immersion with parallax backgrounds, adding a dynamic scrolling effect that elevates your website's visual storytelling.

Add data-parallax and .data-rellax-speed to make a parallax object. 

<h1 data-rellax-speed="-20" data-parallax="data-parallax">Parallax</h1>

data-rellax-speed can be positive or negative number .

JavaScript
<script src="../vendors/rellax/rellax.js"></script>
